Neural Draft LLC ("**Neural Draft**," "**we**," "**us**," or "**our**") operates the Gaijinn software platform, including its command-line interface (CLI), web interfaces, APIs, and related services (collectively, the "**Service**"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you access or use the Service.

By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, do not use the Service.

---

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information we process in connection with:

- Your Neural Draft account and billing relationship;
- Your use of the Gaijinn CLI, supervisor APIs, and associated web properties;
- Communications you send to us; and
- Data you submit or that is generated when the Service analyzes your repositories or executes sprints.

This policy is intended primarily for users located in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the United States, you understand that your information may be processed in the United States and other jurisdictions where we or our service providers operate.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

### 2.1 Account and Contact Information

When you register for or use the Service, we may collect:

- Email address and account identifiers;
- Name or organization name, if provided;
- Authentication credentials and session tokens;
- Billing contact details and transaction references (payment card data is processed by our payment processor, not stored directly by us in full); and
- Communications you send to us, including support requests.

### 2.2 Repository and Project Data

To provide architectural analysis, blueprint generation, and sprint orchestration, the Service may process:

- **Repository snapshots and structural metadata**, including file paths, dependency graphs, import relationships, module boundaries, and derived metrics;
- Configuration files relevant to project analysis;
- Prompts, intents, and blueprint inputs you supply; and
- Outputs generated by the Service, such as gravity/curvature metrics, work-unit decompositions, and sprint artifacts stored under project-local directories (for example, `.gaijinn/`).

We design the core CLI workflow to operate locally where possible. If you use cloud-hosted or API-connected features, corresponding project data may be transmitted to and temporarily stored on our infrastructure for the duration necessary to perform the requested operation.

### 2.3 Sprint and Operational Logs

When you run atomic sprints, grid orchestration, or supervisor-backed workflows, we may collect:

- Sprint start and completion timestamps;
- Worker cell assignments and execution status;
- Integrity scores, complexity indices, and billing-relevant usage metrics;
- Error, diagnostic, and telemetry events necessary to operate, secure, and improve the Service; and
- Aggregated performance data that does not identify you personally when de-identified.

### 2.4 Technical and Usage Information

We automatically collect certain technical data, including:

- IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and operating system;
- CLI version, API request metadata, and feature usage patterns;
- Log files, crash reports, and security event records; and
- Cookies or similar technologies on our websites, where applicable.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service;
- Authenticate users and enforce license and account terms;
- Generate architectural analyses, blueprints, and sprint orchestration;
- Calculate credit consumption, integrity-based pricing, and billing;
- Respond to inquiries and provide customer support;
- Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security incidents;
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements; and
- Develop aggregated or de-identified analytics that do not reasonably identify you.

We do **not** use your repository content to train public or third-party foundation models unless we obtain your separate, explicit consent for that specific purpose.

---

## 4. What We Do Not Do With Your Information

- **We do not sell your personal information.** Neural Draft does not sell, rent, or trade personally identifiable information ("PII") to data brokers or unrelated third parties for their independent marketing purposes.
- **We do not disclose trade secrets or proprietary algorithms** derived from your data except as necessary to provide the Service under contract, as required by law, or with your direction.
- **We do not publicly publish your private repository contents** or sprint logs except where you explicitly configure sharing or export features.

---

## 5. Trade Secret and Confidentiality Protection

Neural Draft treats certain elements of the Gaijinn platform as confidential trade secrets, including proprietary weighting models, gravity engines, decomposition kernels, and related know-how. We implement administrative, technical, and contractual safeguards designed to:

- Restrict internal access to confidential systems and source materials;
- Limit subprocessors and contractors to need-to-know access under confidentiality obligations;
- Segregate customer project data from proprietary model development except as permitted by this policy and your agreements with us; and
- Respond to unauthorized access or disclosure in accordance with applicable law.

Your use of the Service is also governed by our Terms of Service and, where applicable, confidentiality obligations in separate agreements.
