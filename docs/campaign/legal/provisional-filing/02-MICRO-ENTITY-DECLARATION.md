# Micro entity status certification (pro se)

**For USPTO Patent Center at provisional filing.**

Neural Draft LLC intends to claim **micro entity** status if eligible under 37 CFR 1.29.

## Certification checklist

Check each before selecting "Micro entity" in Patent Center:

- [ ] Applicant is a **person** who is an independent inventor, OR a small business concern, OR nonprofit org qualifying under USPTO rules
- [ ] Named inventor has not been named on **more than four** previously filed U.S. patent applications (certain exceptions apply)
- [ ] Applicant's gross income did not exceed the USPTO micro-entity income threshold in the prior calendar year (see current USPTO fee schedule / MPEP 509)
- [ ] Application is **not** obligated to be assigned to an entity that does not qualify for micro/small entity status
- [ ] Applicant has not assigned, granted, or conveyed a license to an entity that would disqualify micro status
